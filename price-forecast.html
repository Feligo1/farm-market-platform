Market Intelligence

Seasonal Factors ? Prices follow seasonal patterns based on planting and harvest cycles

Prices typically increase 15-20% during planting season as supplies decrease.

Weather Impact ? Weather patterns significantly affect crop yields and market prices

Drought conditions can increase prices by up to 30% within 2-3 weeks.

Logistics ? Transport costs and availability affect final market prices

Fuel price increases of 10% typically add 3-5% to final market prices.

Export Demand ? International market demand influences local prices

Regional export demand can cause price spikes of 20-25%.
